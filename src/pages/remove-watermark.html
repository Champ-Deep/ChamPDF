Frequently Asked Questions

What types of watermarks can this remove?

This tool is optimized for corner watermarks like those from NotebookLM and similar AI tools. Quick mode removes annotation-based overlays, while Deep mode redacts content in the specified corner region.

Is my PDF uploaded to a server?

No! All processing happens directly in your browser using WebAssembly. Your PDF never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy.

What's the difference between Quick and Deep mode?

Quick mode removes annotations, form fields, and overlay-type watermarks. It's fast and safe. Deep mode uses content redaction to remove graphic watermarks embedded in the PDF content stream, which is more thorough but takes longer.

Can I add my own logo after removing the watermark?

Yes! You can optionally replace the removed watermark with a company logo (LakeB2B, Champions, or Ampliz). The logo will be placed in the same corner where the watermark was removed.
