Frequently Asked Questions

What watermarks can this remove?

This tool is optimized for removing AI-generated content watermarks like NotebookLM, and similar services that place small logos in video corners.

Are my videos kept on your servers?

No. Videos are processed immediately and deleted from our servers once the download completes. We do not store or retain your video files.

What video formats are supported?

We support MP4, MOV, WebM, and AVI files up to 100MB. The output is always an MP4 file for maximum compatibility.

How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on video length and resolution. Most videos under 50MB are processed in under 2 minutes.
